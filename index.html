About CESRG

The Control & Energy Systems Research Group (CESRG) at COMSATS University Islamabad is dedicated to advanced research in control systems, energy systems, electric vehicles, battery management, optimization, and intelligent systems.

CESRG bridges theoretical research and real-world applications through interdisciplinary collaboration and high-impact publications.
