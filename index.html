Home Page Content (Control & Energy Systems Research Group) Welcome to the Control & Energy Systems Research Group (CESRG) — where theory meets real-world energy innovation. We are a multidisciplinary research lab dedicated to exploring intelligent control, optimal energy management, and sustainable power systems to address some of the most pressing challenges of modern energy infrastructure. Our Mission At CESRG, our mission is to: Advance the frontiers of control theory by developing robust, adaptive, and data-driven algorithms Integrate control strategies with energy systems like batteries, microgrids, and renewable sources Drive sustainable energy solutions that are both efficient and scalable — for both grid-level and embedded applications Research Themes Our research spans a broad spectrum of topics, including (but not limited to): State-of-the-art control methods: nonlinear control, model predictive control, sliding mode, observer design Battery management systems: state-of-charge (SoC) / state-of-health (SoH) estimation, balancing, thermal modeling Energy system optimization: microgrid control, energy storage integration, demand-side management Smart power electronics: converters, bidirectional chargers, grid-connected inverters Sustainable systems: integration of renewable energy sources, energy efficiency, and system-level resilience Why CESRG? Interdisciplinary expertise: Our group brings together electrical engineers, control theorists, and energy system specialists. High impact: Our publications and projects aim to have both academic rigor and real-world relevance — contributing to smarter, cleaner energy. Collaborative environment: We work with industry partners, utilities, and other research labs to turn our control-energy research into practical applications. Training future researchers: We mentor students and early-career researchers, helping them grow into leaders in control and energy domains. What We Offer Publications & tools: Access our latest research papers, software, open-source tools, and datasets. Team & People: Meet our faculty, postdocs, PhD students, and collaborators. News & Updates: Stay informed about our latest projects, grants, conferences, and seminars. Join Us: Interested in collaborating or joining our group? Learn how you can become part of CESRG.
